# Project Manager (IT)

## GENEL BİLGİLER
**Pozisyon Seviyesi:** Mid to Senior Level
**Departman:** PMO (Project Management Office) / IT
**Raporlama:** IT Manager/Director, PMO Director
**Deneyim Gereksinimi:** 5-8 yıl IT deneyimi, 3+ yıl proje yönetimi
**Maaş Aralığı:** 80.000 - 150.000 TL/ay

---

## GÖREVLER VE SORUMLULUKLAR

### Proje Planlama Görevleri
- Proje charter hazırlama ve onay alma
- Proje kapsamını (scope) belirleme ve dokümante etme
- Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) oluşturma
- Proje zaman çizelgesi hazırlama (Gantt chart)
- Kaynak planlaması ve allocation
- Bütçe hazırlama ve cost estimation
- Risk register oluşturma ve risk analizi
- Stakeholder analysis ve communication plan
- Quality management plan hazırlama
- Procurement plan (gerekirse)

### Proje Yürütme Görevleri
- Ekip üyelerine görev dağılımı (task assignment)
- Daily/weekly standup meetings yönetme
- Sprint planning ve sprint review (Agile)
- Proje ilerlemesini takip etme (tracking)
- Issue ve blocker'ları çözme
- Vendor koordinasyonu
- Change request yönetimi
- Quality assurance süreçlerini takip
- Team collaboration sağlama
- Documentation maintenance

### Proje İzleme ve Kontrol
- Proje metriklerini takip etme (KPIs)
- Earned Value Management (EVM) analizi
- Budget variance analysis
- Schedule variance tracking
- Risk monitoring ve mitigation
- Issue log yönetimi
- Status reporting (weekly/monthly)
- Stakeholder communication
- Scope creep kontrolü
- Quality metrics tracking

### Proje Kapanış Görevleri
- Project closure documentation
- Lessons learned session organize etme
- Final deliverables teslimi
- Stakeholder sign-off alma
- Team performance review
- Project archive oluşturma
- Post-implementation review
- Success metrics reporting
- Knowledge transfer
- Celebration ve recognition

### Stakeholder Yönetimi
- Stakeholder expectations yönetimi
- Regular status updates
- Executive presentations
- Conflict resolution
- Escalation management
- Feedback collection
- Relationship building
- Communication planning

## PROJE YÖNETİCİSİ YETKİNLİK MATRİSİ

### Junior PM (0-3 yıl)
- Small projects (<500K TL, <6 months)
- Single team (5-8 people)
- Waterfall or simple Agile
- Close supervision needed

### Mid-Level PM (3-5 yıl)
- Medium projects (500K-2M TL, 6-12 months)
- Multiple teams (10-15 people)
- Agile/Hybrid methodologies
- Minimal supervision

### Senior PM (5-8 yıl)
- Large projects (2M-5M TL, 12-18 months)
- Cross-functional teams (15-25 people)
- Complex Agile/Hybrid
- Independent execution

### Lead PM (8+ yıl)
- Strategic projects (5M+ TL, 18+ months)
- Multiple projects/programs
- Enterprise-wide impact
- Mentoring other PMs

## ÖNEMLİ NOTLAR

**PMP Certification Gereksinimleri:**
- 4-year degree: 36 months PM experience, 35 contact hours education
- High school diploma: 60 months PM experience, 35 contact hours education
- PMP exam: 180 questions, 230 minutes
- Renewal: 60 PDUs every 3 years

**Agile Certification Path:**
1. CSM (Certified Scrum Master) - Entry level
2. CSPO (Certified Scrum Product Owner)
3. CSP (Certified Scrum Professional)
4. PMI-ACP (Agile Certified Practitioner)
5. SAFe Agilist (for enterprise Agile)

**Career Growth Timeline:**
- Years 0-3: Junior PM → PM
- Years 3-5: PM → Senior PM
- Years 5-8: Senior PM → Lead PM
- Years 8+: Lead PM → Program Manager / PMO Director
