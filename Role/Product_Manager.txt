# Product Manager (Technical)

## GENEL BİLGİLER
**Pozisyon Seviyesi:** Mid to Senior Level
**Departman:** Product Management / Engineering
**Raporlama:** VP of Product, CTO, CEO
**Deneyim Gereksinimi:** 5-8 yıl ürün/IT deneyimi, 3+ yıl product management
**Maaş Aralığı:** 100.000 - 180.000 TL/ay

---

## GÖREVLER VE SORUMLULUKLAR

### Ürün Stratejisi Görevleri
- Ürün vizyonu ve stratejisini belirleme
- Product roadmap oluşturma (quarterly, yearly)
- Product-market fit analizi
- Competitive intelligence ve market research
- Go-to-market (GTM) stratejisi geliştirme
- Pricing strategy belirleme
- Product positioning ve messaging
- Product lifecycle management
- Innovation pipeline yönetimi
- Strategic partnerships değerlendirme

### Ürün Planlama Görevleri
- Product backlog oluşturma ve önceliklendirme
- Feature prioritization (RICE, MoSCoW, KANO)
- Release planning (quarterly releases)
- Sprint planning'e katılım
- User story mapping
- Epic ve user story yazma
- Acceptance criteria tanımlama
- Technical requirements documentation
- Product requirements document (PRD) hazırlama
- Roadmap communication

### Kullanıcı Araştırması Görevleri
- User research planning ve execution
- Customer interviews yapma
- User persona oluşturma
- Customer journey mapping
- Usability testing organize etme
- A/B testing tasarlama ve analiz etme
- User feedback toplama ve analiz
- NPS (Net Promoter Score) tracking
- Customer satisfaction surveys
- Beta testing programı yönetme

### Ürün Geliştirme Görevleri
- Engineering team ile daily collaboration
- Sprint planning ve grooming meetings
- User story refinement
- Design review ve approval
- Technical feasibility assessment
- Feature specification yazma
- API requirements tanımlama
- Integration requirements belirleme
- Technical debt önceliklendirme
- Bug triage ve prioritization

### Ürün Lansmanı Görevleri
- Launch plan hazırlama
- Marketing team ile koordinasyon
- Sales enablement materials hazırlama
- Product documentation review
- Training materials oluşturma
- Beta program yönetimi
- Launch metrics tanımlama
- Post-launch monitoring
- Customer onboarding optimization
- Feedback loop oluşturma

### Analitik ve Metrik Görevleri
- Product KPIs tanımlama ve tracking
- Analytics dashboard oluşturma
- User behavior analysis
- Funnel analysis
- Cohort analysis
- Retention analysis
- Churn analysis
- Revenue metrics tracking
- Feature adoption tracking
- Data-driven decision making

### Stakeholder Yönetimi
- Executive stakeholder updates
- Customer advisory board meetings
- Sales team collaboration
- Marketing alignment
- Customer success partnership
- Engineering team alignment
- Design team collaboration
- Support team feedback loop
- External partner communication

## PERFORMANS KRİTERLERİ (KPIs)

### Product Success Metrics
- **Product-Market Fit Score:** 40%+ "very disappointed"
- **NPS (Net Promoter Score):** 50+
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** 4.5+/5.0
- **Feature Adoption Rate:** 60%+ (30 days)
- **Time to Value:** <7 days

### Growth Metrics
- **User Growth:** 20%+ MoM (growth stage)
- **Revenue Growth:** 15%+ QoQ
- **Activation Rate:** 40%+ (new users)
- **Retention Rate:** 80%+ (30-day), 60%+ (90-day)
- **Churn Rate:** <5% monthly

### Engagement Metrics
- **DAU/MAU Ratio:** 30%+
- **Session Duration:** Increasing trend
- **Feature Usage:** 70%+ of core features
- **User Engagement Score:** 4.0+/5.0

### Business Metrics
- **Revenue per User (ARPU):** Increasing trend
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** 3x CAC
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Decreasing trend
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 3:1 or better
- **Payback Period:** <12 months

### Delivery Metrics
- **Roadmap Delivery:** 80%+ on-time
- **Sprint Goal Achievement:** 85%+
- **Feature Release Frequency:** Monthly+
- **Time to Market:** <3 months (major features)
- **Bug Rate:** <5% post-release

### Team & Stakeholder Metrics
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction:** 4.0+/5.0
- **Engineering Team Satisfaction:** 4.0+/5.0
- **Cross-functional Collaboration Score:** 4.0+/5.0
- **Product Vision Clarity:** 4.5+/5.0

- Product experience overview
- Cultural fit
- Salary expectations

- Product design questions

  - "Improve product Y"
- Feature prioritization
- User empathy
- Creativity

### Aşama 3: Analytical/Metrics Interview (60 dakika)
- SQL test (live coding)
- Metrics definition
- A/B test design
- Data interpretation
- Root cause analysis

### Aşama 4: Technical Interview (45 dakika)
- System design (high-level)
- API design
- Technical trade-offs
- Architecture discussions
- Technical feasibility

### Aşama 5: Execution Interview (60 dakika)
- Roadmap planning exercise
- Stakeholder management scenario
- Prioritization exercise
- Go-to-market planning

### Aşama 6: Behavioral Interview (45 dakika)
- Leadership examples
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Conflict resolution

- Cultural fit
